Studying alone? Find your Buddy Today!

Study buddy is a new application designed to help you achieve your goals during your college years. Making friends in your major is vital for a successful college experience, and for students that embark on this journey alone, sometimes it can be tough to find buddies in their classes. Study Buddy is here to help you make long lasting connections and to help make your transition and involvement in classes smoother.

Services

Our Services

Study Buddy is equipped with all the necessary utilities to help make your experience as effortless as possible.

Profile

Each user will have the disposal of its own unique profile. Profiles contain basic user information such as its email address and phone number.

Subject Select

A complete catalog of all courses offered in SU will be available so each user can select the courses he/she is interested in studying for.

Buddy Search

This service allows users to look and select the appropriate buddy for him. The subject and proficiency test factor in.

Invitation

Users will be able to send Invitations to their selected buddy and he/she will have the ability to deny or accept the request.

Materials

Users will be allowed to share materials such as documents, videos, pictures â€¦ to selected buddies corresponding to a selected subject.

Rating

Users will be able to leave a review and rating of their buddy after each study session to track academical improvement.
